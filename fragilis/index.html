Fragilis

Nothing breaks like a heart

I'm the kind of person that when it gets obsses with a song, it listen it in loop for hours, sometimes days. Yesterday, a new song got in.

It's called Crystal Children by Le Metroid. This particular song not only was in loop but I didn't sleep my usual quota. Besides that, I was crying all the time. I have no idea why.

I emailed the creator. I was so overwhelmed that I wanted to thank him/her. It's been a long time since a song causes something like that in me.

It also reminds me of her. She is so fragile. She feels a lot. I can barely make sense of it. Fragilis.

I don't mean as a weakness but rather as something beautiful. For some reason, it's like observing the universe itself unfolding in front of my eyes and I'm so lucky to witness such thing.
