Can You Say One Thing That's True

One of the hardest thing in life, I think, is being yourself. At most we have this set of persona to navigate our social interactions. And the reason is obvious we all want to be accepted.

Now imagine those with hard traumas. Certainly most of them are in jail. Poor twisted souls, can you blame them? Those in a bit lower category, are in another kind of prison, the looney bin. The rest, we are free, trying to survive and dealing with profund terror of all things not to mention our own personal traumas. Sometimes I wonder what's all the point of this? Is there even a reason?

Oh well. I believe there is but that's for another time.

For now, I'll be waiting for the twisted soul sent to bring me back to life.
