Glia

The term glia from Greek word meaning glue was a presumption that these cells held the nervous system together in some way. There has been any evidence of such thing.

For me, that's the name I want for my first daughter. And of course I just want this to happen with one and only person. 

Late I've been missing her quite a lot. I just can get her out of my mind, body, spirit and soul. Her words are still dancing in my heart and filling it with such joy. Even though I have so many reason to just let go and go away. I just can't.

I've been asking myself why I love her so darn much and in such a way it does make any sense. I started by removing one thing at the time and in the end, I still was loving her the same. I removed personality traits. Nothing change. I removed physical traits. Still the same. I removed virtues and values. Still bloody the same. I started to going mad. What was it, huh? I remember the quote from Jamie Lannister from the show:

We don't get to choose who we love that's beyond our control

And that should be the same for a hopeless romantic and mystical person of me. But I couldn't let go. Then suddenly it came to me: I've been removing all good things. Just good things.

So I started now to remove the bad traits and so and then... it started to feel different. I realized I love her primarily because of her bad traits and so on. I used to say to her: I love you as a whole you and your shadow, I want all of it.

Now, I know for sure that it is the bad traits... 

I guess I just went nuts.
