Heka

When first we met, it felt out of this world
Such depth of feeling in her eyes did glow
Our bond grew strong, though troubles were unfurled
We each faced fears that only love could know

Through trials harsh, our hearts began to mend
And perseverance marked our troubled way
One day she spoke the word that would transcend
“We are soulmates,” in her voice did say

Complete am I, though paths diverge in time
For in her love, I found my spirit whole
Her life's own course may differ from my climb
Yet I am one with God, and peace my goal

In love and faith, my soul finds calm and grace
Her lasting words, my heart will e'er embrace
