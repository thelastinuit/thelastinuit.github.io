There Is No Reason To Be Afraid. Ever.

Laß dir Alles geschehn: Schönheit und Schrecken.
Man muß nur gehn: Kein Gefühl ist das fernste. 

When the darkness falls, all I can think of is all the mistakes I have done. All the damage I have caused. And the perfect storm enters again in my life. There is so much to feel, so much to suffer. But today the opposite happens.

Today while I was listening to John Denver's Annie's Song, I just started crying and felt so much love. All I can think of was how everything is so beautiful. Of course I remember her. And more joy came to my heart. I felt my heart about to explode. My body was filled with such awe. I just stopped working and enjoyed the moment. 

There is no reason to be afraid. Ever.
