!Naive + Trust = Courage

One of the things I believe is to go around with an open chest, you have my full trust  even if you are a total stranger specially then. People have questioned that before as you are stupid or at the very least naive. I disagree.

You know, I think I'm stronger enough to take a hit. I'm not naive enough to know that everyone can hurt you. Of course I know. I'm aware. I was not born yesterday. But you see, that's ok. I think everyone has their own journey. Maybe that was not the time for us to start a friendship or relationship. You hurt me and that's ok. I don't blame you at all. But I do have so little time on this planet that I cannot invest more time with ya. I wish ya the best and no hard feelings.

I believe in long lasting relationships. I think we all do. I think to put your faith on someone knowing they can hurt ya is courageus. You know that things can go sideways intentionally or unintentionally. That's alright. I do think this is the best strategy to move around in our society. We are not in the jungle anymore... well, maybe we are but I think this is better than any alternative.

I practice this in my daily life. I believe it and I live it. This is the way I am and the leader I want to be for my community, my civilization. This is what I owe to myself. This is after all my journey.

Believe in human beings specially nowadays.

Give it a try.

You may get surprised.

Now more than ever, we need the Jedi.
