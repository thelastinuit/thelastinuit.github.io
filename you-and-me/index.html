It Is You and Me

In the moments of despair, whether or not those are common among humans, I find myself I'm fine. That is not because I enjoy it but because the nature of my upbringing. You see, I am a poorly-made human being. I cannot speak about others, maybe everyone is. But I see other people well adjusted and makes me wonder. What's wrong with me?

Why do I feel too much that simple words of rejection tear apart my little wasted hearth? Why do I feel lonely even though I'm surrounded by people who clearly love me for who I am? Why do I always find a way to run away from things that bring me joy? Why do I enjoy suffering? Is it because I deserve it? Is this just my burden that I cannot share with anyone? How do couples are made and persist? I have failed so hard in my life and I thought I knew why. I have asked for forgiveness for my stupidity and my issues. I have forgave myself for always believing it is all my fault. I did have a piece of the cake but I always blame myself for everything like I don't deserve anything.

I do sacrifice myself for others in hopes of being accepted, loved and cared for. I think I always be regardless of the reason. This is my way of living. However, I will no longer run away. I will confront my fear of abandonment and rejection which is symbolically speaking to confront my death.

I am so sorry for all the pain I have caused you. After all the pain and suffering, you persisted, you have always had me. I think it's time for me to show you the same.

'Cuz God only knows what I'd be without you...
