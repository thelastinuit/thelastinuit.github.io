Call My Bullshit

If you see me fail at not showing the following traits, please call my bullshit. 

low-key: never seeking or look to receive much attention or recognition.
lack of self-confidence: to doubt about me all the time as a default, then perhaps not.
zero yelling: to not to raise my voice.
sheer will: to never give up especially when my actions are futile.
compassionate: always be kindhearted even when everything is saying otherwise.
humble: above all, always be.
charisma: exude as much as possible.
fearless: fear is fine but never let it stop you. ever.
