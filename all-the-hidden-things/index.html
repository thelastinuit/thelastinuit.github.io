All The Hidden Things

All the hidden things your brain keeps away from you, it's 'cuz it wants to protect you. All those memories you know they are there but you cannot recall them. All those emotions of annihilation and other bad feelings. It is only when the right trigger happens, that the vault is open and everything happens. All. At. Once.

It is in that moment when you see your demons. When you recall all the abuse you went through. When you see the person you are is not what you were but merely a construct  you create to survive. 

But one day the right person will come. The one who will open the vault whether she knows it or not. And she sees you all. And only two things can happen. Either she goes away or she stays.

We are not monsters but we can become one. I believe, with the right partner, you can tame that monster inside you and become more powerful you can ever imagine. You can do good in society. I know you can. I wanted to believe you can do that alone... but as far as I can tell, we need each other.

For me, I hope she stays but also know that if she leaves, she will be happy.
