My personal translation is the principle of not forcing. And in this particular post, it's about control. Never thought I wanted to control things. I sincerely believe I was not. But, it turned out, I'm just like any other human being. Control gives us security and we all need that.

After a few decades on this beautiful planet, yesterday, I learnt I have to let it go. I have to stop controlling things and let it happen in whichever way they need to be. 

Because I believe now the best way to move forward is by having faith and let life flow. It is the optimist who gets to be rich, the cynic gets to be right. And in being so, I want to be optimistic and let life to teach me to be cynic if that's its will.

The great things you can have in life will costs everything. EVERYTHING.

Gianella, this is for you. You will cost me everything. Nonetheless, your mere existence is my every single thing. 

For all the mistakes I have made...

For what it's worth, I love you...
