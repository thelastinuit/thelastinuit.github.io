The Unstoppable Force

I'm deeply in love with her. I've been there for her in a terrible times, I've been there for her in other ways too. Will I be for her tomorrow? Next year? Yes. a thousand years, yes. Just like Ted Mosby said:

If you’re looking for the word that means caring about someone beyond all rationality and wanting them to have everything they want no matter how much it destroys you, it’s love. And when you love someone you don’t stop, ever. Even when people roll their eyes, and call you crazy. Even then. Especially then.

But I have to be honest. I'm very transparent on how I feel about her. How I look at her. How I deal with her. And I know her friends and family notice it. It's just a matter of time that she will fall in love with someone and that someone will see how unbelievable and increible she is. When that day comes, I'll be quite happy for her because love is the unstoppable force which means... I've got to move.

Even after all this time
The Sun never says to the Earth
"You owe me"
Look what happens with a love like that
