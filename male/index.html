Only the most broken people can be great leaders
Namor

Ain't no kind of person who has strong opinions about anything. I collect questions and soft opinions. I'm not married to any opinion of mine. I try to understand stuff and in the end I follow my belief system even when it does not match with reality. So, for the first time, I will make a strong opinion: A male, a true male, is here to protect and serve.

In terms of biology, a male is the hunter, the provider, the protector. I like to believe that's the same in terms of, hmm, spiritual energy. In order to be the best leader, serving others is the only way.

Along the same topic, being part of the community involves your sacrifice. There is no community without sacrifice. Thus, a male in community by definition and nature, is the one who protect and serves its community.

Now, who would be a better leader? I believe now that the best leader is the one who didn't have any love. The one who was rejected. The one who was abandoned. The one who has such a shadow, the hardest one, I think. Those who were there but conquered their shadow, are the best leader. Where are they? One place, I think, is orphan houses. 

If they can conquer their shadows, I believe, they will be the best leaders. It is not an easy job for sure but I think it's worth trying.
