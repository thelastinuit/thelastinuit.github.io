I Don't Understand What's Going On Most Of The Time

I have this tendency to always take the blame as a starter. I think it makes things easier for me. I can control if I can fix something or at least to try to fix something.

Not only reality is very complicate (how do we even understand the sense of being? Do we though?), we have different languages and communication barriers. Everyone has its own understanding of words. We all have our own perceptions of events and yet all of them are in a sense real. I don't understand what's going on most of the time.

I spent most my life trying to decode human behaviour. I have read quite a few books in psychology, neurology and languages. None of them I have prepared for what living right now. And I don't want to fail her... I love her very much.

I must recognize that I have no much experience in romantic relationships. Perhaps that's part of the problem. But I made the decision since I was very young that I will always continue to fight to make a relationship work unless either two things happen:

  1. She says I have to leave her alone.
  2. I see that me staying causes more troubles than going away.

Which in turned did not provide much experience.

Human behavior is very interesting. I made peace that I will never understand it but I've gotta keep trying especially when it comes to her.

It hurts a lot to see her crying. Of course, I take the blame, it's my nature. Somehow, it feels like it's all my fault. And I just want to be a force of good to her, to care of her, to support her, to be for her and yet it feels I'm failing again and again.

I just hope that neither of those options happen but if it does, I know it will be for the best for her.

Me? Well, I'll probably be not ok but I have a few plans that they will keep me busy.

I can't blame no one but myself.

You know who you are. I promise I will be there for you and to try to be better for you every day unless, of course, you desire otherways.

Ich liebe dich so sehr... 😔
