But If It's Me Reading The Signs

At my age, or any age for that matter, I'm sure people will say it's silly to believe in signs from the Universe, God, or however you want to call it. I believe and follow of them since I was a child. As long as my intuition and heart was in aligned, I always, always follow them.

Of course with time, I learnt that it didn't mean it was going to be a good thing, nor even a bad thing. But it'd be a path that you needed to take in order to learn, or fail to learn, a lesson.

I have made so many great mistakes. I have hurt people deeply. I've been hurt by people deeply. I regret none of it. Some of them became my best friends. Some other, never came back. That's alright. I didn't mean to hurt them and I know they didn't mean to hurt me. That's just the way it is and I'm ok with the logic behind.

And evidently it is intertwined with my romantic way of being. Both are silly beliefs I won't deny that. I love that part of me, though. 

I just hope one day she, whoever she is, if there is any, can beat my crazy by doing something crazy herself.

Thus then, I will tell her thank you, I love you.
